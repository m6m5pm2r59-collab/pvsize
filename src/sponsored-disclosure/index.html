Sponsored Content & Affiliate Disclosure

Last updated: June 25, 2026

1. How PVSize Makes Money

PVSize is a free resource. We do not charge readers for access to our calculators, guides, or data. To sustain our operations while keeping content free, we generate revenue through:

  • Clearly labeled sponsored placements for solar brands or equipment providers
  • Opt-in lead partnerships where users explicitly consent before project information is shared
  • Future affiliate or commerce partnerships only when clearly disclosed and relevant

None of these revenue sources influence our editorial decisions. We maintain a strict firewall between our commercial and editorial operations.

3. Affiliate and Commerce Disclosure

PVSize keeps user-facing calculators free and open. Future affiliate or commerce links may be evaluated only when they are clearly disclosed, relevant to the page, and separated from calculator outputs.

Our commitment: partner or affiliate relationships must never influence calculator results, factual explanations, or editorial conclusions.

4. Mandatory Disclosure Banner

Any PVSize page that contains sponsored, affiliate, or partner-linked content must display a clear disclosure before the relevant content:

This banner appears on articles containing sponsored, partner-linked, or affiliate-linked content. It is never hidden, collapsed, or placed below the fold.

5. What We Don't Do

  • We do not accept payment to alter, remove, or inflate product ratings
  • We do not publish "advertorials" disguised as independent reviews
  • We do not use affiliate links in calculator results or data visualizations
  • We do not place affiliate links in the /insights/china-brands/ B2B section (kept ad-free for credibility with manufacturer partners)
