Sponsored Content & Affiliate Disclosure

Last updated: June 25, 2026

1. How PVSize Makes Money

PVSize is a free resource. We do not charge readers for access to our calculators, guides, or data. To sustain our operations while keeping content free, we generate revenue through:

  • Display advertising (Google AdSense and direct-sold placements)
  • Affiliate commissions (Amazon Associates and similar programs)
  • B2B services for solar manufacturers (lead generation, market insights, Amazon listing operation)

None of these revenue sources influence our editorial decisions. We maintain a strict firewall between our commercial and editorial operations.

3. Amazon Associates Disclosure

PVSize is a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to Amazon.com and affiliated sites.

When you click an Amazon affiliate link on PVSize and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission — at no additional cost to you. These commissions help fund our research and content production.

Our commitment: Amazon affiliate links never influence product rankings, recommendations, or editorial conclusions. We link to products based on merit, not commission rates.

4. Mandatory Disclosure Banner

Every PVSize article that contains affiliate links displays the following disclosure banner at the top of the page, before the article body:

Affiliate Disclosure: PVSize is reader-supported. When you buy through links on our site, we may earn an affiliate commission — at no extra cost to you. Learn more.

This banner appears on articles containing Amazon Associates links, product recommendation widgets, or any other affiliate-linked content. It is never hidden, collapsed, or placed below the fold.

5. What We Don't Do

  • We do not accept payment to alter, remove, or inflate product ratings
  • We do not publish "advertorials" disguised as independent reviews
  • We do not use affiliate links in calculator results or data visualizations
  • We do not place affiliate links in the /insights/china-brands/ B2B section (kept ad-free for credibility with manufacturer partners)
