Why PVSize?

Data-Driven. We use public utility-rate references, solar-yield assumptions, and panel specification inputs to produce planning estimates. Where a calculation depends on a source or assumption, we show that context on the relevant calculator page.

Free & Transparent. No signup, no installer calls, no hidden fees. We provide the numbers and let you make informed decisions — whether you're sizing panels for your roof, calculating battery storage, or estimating your carbon offset.
