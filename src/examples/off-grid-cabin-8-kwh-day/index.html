PVSize off-grid guide

Solar and battery sizing for an off-grid cabin using 8 kWh/day

A full example for a small cabin where winter sun and backup margin matter more than annual average output.

Load and weather assumptions

  • Daily energy use: 8 kWh/day
  • Winter sun assumption: 3 peak sun-hours/day
  • System loss factor: 25%
  • Backup target: 2 days
  • Battery chemistry: LiFePO4 at 90% DoD

Sizing result

PV array
3.6 kW
Rated battery
17.8 kWh
Inverter planning
3-5 kW

PV array formula: 8 kWh/day divided by 3 winter sun-hours divided by 0.75 system efficiency = about 3.6 kW. Battery formula: 8 kWh/day x 2 backup days divided by 0.90 DoD = about 17.8 kWh rated capacity.
