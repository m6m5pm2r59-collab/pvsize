Planning total: about 5 kWh usable. With LiFePO4 at 90% depth of discharge, rated capacity should be about 5.6 kWh before inverter and installation constraints.

Why usable and rated capacity differ

A battery advertised as 10 kWh does not always mean 10 kWh should be used in every outage. Chemistry, allowed depth of discharge, inverter losses, temperature, and reserve settings all affect usable energy.
