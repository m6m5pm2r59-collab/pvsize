How many solar panels for 1,500 kWh per month?

A transparent worked example for a high-usage home. Change the sun-hours, panel wattage, and roof assumptions in the calculator link below.

Scenario

  • Monthly electricity use: 1,500 kWh
  • Daily electricity use: about 50 kWh/day
  • Peak sun-hours assumption: 5.0 hours/day
  • System loss factor: 20%
  • Panel size: 400W residential panels

Calculation

Daily load
50 kWh
Estimated system
12.5 kW
400W panels
32 panels

Formula: 50 kWh/day divided by 5 sun-hours divided by 0.80 system efficiency = about 12.5 kW DC. With 400W panels, that is about 32 panels before roof layout constraints.

A 400W panel often needs roughly 18-20 sq ft. With spacing and roof setbacks, this example should be checked against usable roof area, not total roof area.
