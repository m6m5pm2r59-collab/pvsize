How Many Solar Panels for a Remote Cabin?

A remote cabin can use a small solar system if loads are controlled, but battery sizing is just as important as panel count. Start with lights, fridge, water pump, internet, and seasonal use.

Short answer: weekend cabins may need only a modest array, while full-time cabins need a full off-grid design with backup days.

Inputs to List

  • Daily kWh for essential appliances.
  • Weekend or full-time occupancy.
  • Battery backup days and generator fallback.
  • Winter or rainy-season production limits.
