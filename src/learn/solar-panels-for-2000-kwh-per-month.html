How Many Solar Panels for 2,000 kWh per Month?

A home using 2,000 kWh per month is a high-usage case. A planning estimate may land around a 14-20 kW solar system, often about 35-50 panels depending on panel wattage and climate.

Short answer: for 2,000 kWh/month, roof space and utility policy can become the limiting factors, not just panel count.

What to Check First

  • Enough usable roof or ground-mount space.
  • Whether the local utility allows the target system size.
  • Battery needs if high usage includes outages, heat pumps, or EV charging.
  • Seasonal production differences in your region.
