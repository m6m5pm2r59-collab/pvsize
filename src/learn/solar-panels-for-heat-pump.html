How Many Solar Panels for a Heat Pump?

A heat pump can reduce fossil-fuel heating, but it adds electric load. Solar sizing should use seasonal kWh, climate, insulation, and whether the heat pump runs mostly in winter, summer, or both.

Global planning note: cold climates may need more winter energy, while warm climates may overlap heat pump cooling with strong solar production.

Inputs to Check

  • Heat pump annual or seasonal kWh.
  • Local heating and cooling season length.
  • Home insulation and thermostat settings.
  • Battery or grid backup expectations.
