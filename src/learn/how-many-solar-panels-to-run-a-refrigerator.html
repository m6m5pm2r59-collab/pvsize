How Many Solar Panels to Run a Refrigerator?

For a refrigerator alone, 1-3 panels can often cover the daily energy use in a planning estimate, but the exact number depends on the fridge's wattage, compressor cycling, sun hours, and whether you also want battery backup for nighttime.

Short answer: most modern fridges use about 1-2 kWh/day, so a small solar setup can usually cover it on paper.

What Changes the Number?

  • Fridge efficiency: newer Energy Star models use less power.
  • Daily runtime: compressor cycling changes consumption.
  • Location and weather: cloudy periods reduce production.
  • Battery need: a fridge needs power when the sun is down unless grid-tied or battery-backed.
