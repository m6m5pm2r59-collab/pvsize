How Many Solar Panels for 500 kWh per Month?

A home using 500 kWh per month may need roughly a 3.5-5 kW solar system in many climates. With common residential panels, that often means about 8-14 panels.

Global planning note: the same 500 kWh bill needs fewer panels in high-sun regions and more panels in cloudy or high-latitude regions.

What Changes the Estimate?

  • Local peak sun hours and seasonal weather.
  • Panel wattage and real-world system losses.
  • Roof orientation, shade, and available mounting area.
  • Whether you want partial or near-total bill offset.
