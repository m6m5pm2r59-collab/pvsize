What Size Solar System Do I Need for My Home?

The best first estimate comes from your monthly kWh usage, not your country alone. A smaller home with electric heating or EV charging can need more solar than a larger efficient home.

Simple path: monthly kWh -> daily kWh -> local sun hours -> system size in kW -> panel count and roof fit.

Global Inputs

  • Monthly electricity usage from your bill.
  • Local sun hours and seasonal weather.
  • Panel wattage and roof orientation.
  • Battery backup, EV charging, or heat pump loads.
