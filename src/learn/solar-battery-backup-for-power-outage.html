Solar Battery Backup for Power Outages

A solar battery backup plan should start with essential loads, not whole-home guesses. Fridge, lights, router, medical devices, and a few outlets may need far less battery than running central AC for days.

Short answer: many homes start planning around 10-20 kWh of usable battery for essentials, but outage duration and load choices can change that quickly.

What to List Before Sizing

  • Essential appliances: fridge, lights, internet, garage door, well pump, or medical equipment.
  • Outage duration: overnight backup is different from two-day resilience.
  • Solar recharge: daytime solar can extend backup if the system supports islanding.
  • Large loads: air conditioning, electric heat, and EV charging can dominate battery size.
