How Many Solar Panels for a 2,500 Sq Ft House?

A 2,500 sq ft house often has higher usage than a smaller home, so a planning range may be around 18-32 panels. The real driver is monthly kWh, not square footage alone.

Short answer: start by checking your electric bill, then use roof area and panel wattage to see whether the target system fits.

Why Square Footage Is Only a Starting Point

  • Usage varies: HVAC, pool pumps, EV charging, and electric heating can change the result.
  • Roof fit matters: a larger home may still have shade, dormers, vents, or limited south-facing area.
  • Panel choice matters: high-wattage panels can reduce count while keeping system size similar.
