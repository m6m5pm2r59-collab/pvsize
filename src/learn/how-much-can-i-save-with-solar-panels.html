How Much Can I Save With Solar Panels?

Solar savings depend on your electricity usage, utility rate, system size, and location. A planning estimate can show a rough annual savings range, but the real number changes with your bill and local rate structure.

Short answer: many homeowners save hundreds to a few thousand dollars per year, but the actual result depends on usage and utility rate.

What Changes the Savings?

  • Electricity rate: higher rates usually mean larger bill savings.
  • System size: bigger systems can offset more of your usage.
  • Sun hours and roof fit: location and roof layout affect production.
  • Policy: net metering and export credits change the payback story.
