How Many Solar Panels for 1,000 kWh per Month?

A home using 1,000 kWh per month often needs roughly a 7-10 kW solar system in a planning estimate. With modern panels, that can mean about 16-25 panels, depending on panel wattage and local sun hours.

Short answer: start with 1,000 kWh/month, then adjust for location, roof space, shade, and system losses before requesting quotes.

Why the Range Changes

  • Peak sun hours: Arizona may need fewer panels than a cloudier northern city.
  • Panel wattage: higher-wattage panels reduce panel count, but not necessarily total roof fit constraints.
  • System losses: inverter, temperature, wiring, and orientation losses usually reduce real output.
  • Offset goal: some homes target 80% bill offset, while others try to offset nearly all usage.
