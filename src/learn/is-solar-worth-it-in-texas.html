Is Solar Worth It in Texas?

In Texas, solar can be worth it when your utility rate is high enough, your roof fits enough panels, and you want a planning estimate for bill savings or backup power. The answer depends on usage, location, and how much of your home load solar can cover.

Short answer: Texas solar can pencil out well for many homes, but the result depends on rate, roof, usage, and local policy.

What Changes the Answer?

  • Electricity rate: the higher your rate, the more savings solar can offset.
  • Usage pattern: homes with high daytime or annual usage often get more value.
  • Roof fit: usable roof space limits the size of the system.
  • Backup needs: batteries matter if outages or resilience are a priority.
